Vidhya Jyothi English High School

Vidhya Jyothi School started in 1982. It has two branches one in BTM and the other in Hongasandra. The Founder is J Haridasan, who built each brick of this school with great care and love and now it has altogether 3000 students enrolled here to make up their future. The school is in a calm area which help students to concentrate more in study matters and all the facilities like lab, sport, physical activities, library are all provided. Each student will be taken care by the teachers with special interest, so that to make a student with over all improvement. This school with the composite college have a record of 100% results. everyone are treated equally here with no discrimination of anything. so come and join this school to make your future a bright one.
